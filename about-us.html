About Us

Welcome to Dr. Eman Mina Clinic

Welcome to our clinic, where your health and well-being are our top priorities. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing exceptional care tailored to your individual needs. We strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment where you can feel comfortable discussing your health concerns. Our clinic offers a wide range of services, including preventive care, routine check-ups, advanced diagnostics, and specialized treatments.

We believe in the importance of continuity of care and encourage regular visits to maintain optimal health. Thank you for choosing our clinic as your healthcare provider. We are dedicated to partnering with you on your journey to better health and well-being. Your trust and satisfaction are our greatest rewards.

Dr. Eman Mina, Head of Clinic

Eman Mina, MD

Dr. Eman Mina studied medicine in Egypt at Alexandria University graduating in 1993. She completed her internship from the University Hospital of Alexandria in 1995 with special emphasis in Ophthalmology, Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, and OB/GYN. She moved to the United States 1998 and worked as a Medical Interpreter at the M.D. Anderson Cancer Center in Houston, TX and at Houston Neuro Monitoring. She completed her Research Fellowship in 2005 from the University of Texas Health Science Center in San Antonio and her Residency in Family and Community Medine in 2008. Dr. Mina opened her own practice in 2008 and has been dedicated to providing the best, individualized care of each of her patients. Dr. Mina will spend the time listening to your concerns to ensure you receive the attention and care you deserve.
